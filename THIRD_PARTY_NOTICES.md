# Third-Party Notices

## ESCO — European Skills, Competences, Qualifications and Occupations

This service uses the ESCO classification of the European Commission.

The ESCO data bundled with this project is a **modified and adapted version** of the
original ESCO classification v1.2.1. Specifically, it is a curated subset of
occupations, skills, and competences exported to YAML format for use within this
tool. It does not represent the complete ESCO classification.

The original ESCO classification can be downloaded, used, reproduced and reused
for any purpose and by any interested party free of charge, in accordance with
the Commission Decision of 12 December 2011 on the reuse of Commission documents
(2011/833/EU).

Source: https://esco.ec.europa.eu

## O*NET — Occupational Information Network

This product includes data from the O*NET database, sponsored by the
U.S. Department of Labor, Employment and Training Administration (USDOL/ETA).

The O*NET data bundled with this project is a curated subset of the original
O*NET 29.0 database. It does not represent the complete O*NET database.
